What is it
==========

.. topic:: Purpose

    Purpose here


An magnetic dipole is an infinitesimal loop of wire of area :math:`S` carrying a current :math:`I`. The strength of the dipole is given by the magnetic dipole moment (:math:`\mathbf{m}`)

.. math::
	\mathbf{m} = \mathbf{I}S,
